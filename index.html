Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need any experience to start? +
Not at all. Many students come in as complete beginners. We love working with new players — there are no bad habits to unlearn and the progress comes quickly.
What ages do you teach? +
We teach students of all ages — from children (8+) to adults. There is no upper age limit and it is truly never too late to start learning music.
Do I need my own instrument? +
Yes, you will need to have your own instrument for lessons. Do you need help finding one? We can help direct you in the right direction within many options.
Are lessons in-person or online? +
We offer both. In-person lessons take place in Stockton and are recommended. Online lessons are conducted over Zoom, Google Meet or FaceTime video calls should you prefer.
How do I cancel or pause lessons? +
Just let us know 72 hours in advance and the lesson can be rescheduled. We don't believe in locking people into contracts. Life happens — we get it. You can pause for up to 4 weeks. Lessons cancelled within 72 hours of scheduled lesson time will have a $25 cancellation fee and will not be rescheduled
Is the trial lesson really free? +
Yes, completely free with no commitment. We offer a free 30-minute introductory lesson so you can meet your teacher, get a feel for the environment, and make sure it's the right fit before you spend anything.
